HOW TO SEND THE INVITATION EMAIL IN GMAIL
=========================================

1. Open "invitation-email.html" in a web browser
   (double-click the file, or drag it into a browser window)

2. Select everything on the page
   Windows: Ctrl + A
   Mac:     Cmd + A

3. Copy
   Windows: Ctrl + C
   Mac:     Cmd + C

4. Open Gmail and click "Compose" to start a new email

5. Click inside the email body and paste
   Windows: Ctrl + V
   Mac:     Cmd + V
   The formatted invitation should appear with colors and layout intact.

6. Fill in the To: field with the guest's email address
   Send one at a time, or use BCC to send to multiple guests at once
   without revealing everyone's email address to each other.

7. Set the subject line — suggested:
   "You're Invited — Lorraine's 90th Birthday"

8. Hit Send.


NOTES
-----
- Do not forward the email to yourself first — copy fresh from the
  browser each time, or the formatting may degrade.
- If the layout looks broken after pasting, make sure you are pasting
  into the email body (not the subject line) and that Gmail's
  formatting toolbar is visible (not plain-text mode).
- To switch Gmail out of plain-text mode: in the compose window, click
  the three-dot menu at the bottom right and uncheck "Plain text mode".
